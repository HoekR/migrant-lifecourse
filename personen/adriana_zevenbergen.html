28-4-1958

Selection

The family was described as "an excellent family group". That was the reason why Adriana was chosen to be the 100.000th emigrant. During the selection procedure, Cornelis was advised to state in his last employment certificate that he worked as an engineering fitter and not as a plant operator, which might allowed him to change his worker classification as such. Cornelis had still not received this certificate on board the Johan van Oldenbarnevelt. more info
